1. To start, click anywhere on the "Start Here" mat to place the ball.
2. To aim, move aiming balls in the direction you want to swing by moving the mouse.
3. The distance between the aiming balls indicates strength of the swing (far for hard hits, close for soft hits.)

Note: Bonus points are awarded for bankshots. The more banks, the better the bonus.
Also, if you are too close to the edge, you may bank it off the edge to get better distance.
